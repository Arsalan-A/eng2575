Features

Features

Premium Sound

Vibra has been built with a powerful 40mm dynamic driver and utilizes a cutting-edge polymer diaphragm for distortion-free bass and a balanced soundstage.

Auto Pause & Play

You’ll never miss a beat with the Auto Pause and Play feature. With built-in infrared proximity sensor, the audio pauses automatically when you take off the headphones, then resumes when you put them back on

Charging

No more wire hassle! The Vibra Wireless Charging Stand adopts magnetic power contact technology. It is an elegant stainless steel stand that not only showcases your Vibra headphones but is also a charging station that keeps Vibra always ready to rock.

Pairing

Powered by Qualcomm Apt-X LL codec and CSR Bluetooth® 5.0, Vibra elevates every nuance of sound into even finer detail. The latest Bluetooth® 5.0 results in a ‘better-than-CD’ listening experience and clear calls.

App Control

Vibra Premium headset can be controlled by Application and sounnd levels can be adjusted to desried levels.
